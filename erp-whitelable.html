White-label business platform

A multi-module ERP built for SMEs, distributors, manufacturers, and service businesses.

Letsdo ERP is a cloud-ready, multi-tenant business platform that centralizes commercial, financial, operational, and administrative workflows in one branded system. It is designed for companies that need stronger control than spreadsheets and disconnected tools can provide.

The current product already exposes structured admin and transactional modules for tenant setup, organization management, users, roles, audit visibility, CRM, sales, purchase, inventory, finance, HR, payroll, manufacturing, projects, service, assets, documents, approvals, notifications, and analytics.

Offer your own ERP brand with real workflow depth: lead-to-cash, procure-to-pay, inventory movement, payroll processing, production execution, service tracking, and financial reporting.
