White-label AI SaaS platform

A branded AI chatbot and lead management platform built for agencies, SaaS operators, and service businesses.

Letsdo Chatbot CRM is a multi-tenant SaaS product that helps businesses deploy an AI chatbot on their website, train it on company-specific content, capture qualified leads from live conversations, and manage follow-up from a central dashboard.

The product structure already covers business onboarding, knowledge ingestion, embeddable widget delivery, real-time conversations, lead pipeline tracking, team access, workspace analytics, billing visibility, and super-admin controls for platform-wide management.

Offer your own chatbot SaaS brand with website AI chat, knowledge-based answers, lead capture, CRM-style follow-up, billing, and admin controls in one deployable product.

Feature highlights
