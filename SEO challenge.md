# SEO Challenge

## 30-Day SEO Action Plan

### Week 1: Foundation Fixes

1. Fix metadata and H1 mismatches on all main service pages.
2. Add proper `LocalBusiness` or `ProfessionalService` schema site-wide.
3. Standardize NAP (name, address, phone) and India / Coimbatore targeting across core pages.

### Week 2: Landing Pages and Structure

4. Build 5-10 high-intent local and service landing pages.
5. Improve internal linking between homepage, services, products, blog, and contact.
6. Add FAQ sections and FAQ schema to money pages.

### Week 3: Content and Authority

7. Create 6-8 SEO articles around buyer intent.
8. Add case studies and testimonials with real outcomes.

### Week 4: Local SEO and Tracking

9. Optimize Google Business Profile and local citations.
10. Track results in Google Search Console and GA4.

## Suggested Priority Order

- Fix technical and on-page SEO issues first.
- Strengthen local SEO signals next.
- Publish supporting landing pages and articles after the foundation is stable.
- Add proof assets like testimonials and case studies to improve trust and conversions.
- Monitor performance weekly and adjust based on impressions, clicks, rankings, and lead quality.

## Goal

Build a stronger local and service-driven SEO presence for `letsdocreative.com`, improve crawl quality, increase search visibility for high-intent keywords, and turn more organic traffic into qualified leads.
